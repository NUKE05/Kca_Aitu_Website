BULTAOREUNE

BULTAOREUNE(BRN) stands as a vibrant sub-club within the illustrious KCA community wholly committed to the art of dance. This electrifying enclave welcomes every AITU student to partake in its fiery passion for dance and offers them the thrilling opportunity to grace the stage at a multitude of events. The name Bultaoreune finds its inspiration in the iconic song Fire by the legendary K-POP group, BTS. In Korean, it translates to "on fire" or "burning up," perfectly encapsulating the fervor and dynamism that define the essence of this dance-centric sub-club.

HOW TO JOIN BRN?

BULTAOREUNE Igniting the Stage with 100 Strong Members! Join the electrifying experience of BULTAOREUNE, a dynamic dance team that takes center stage at various University events.

REQUIREMENTS TO JOIN BULTAOREUNE:

  1. Be a student of AITU to be eligible for BULTAOREUNE membership.
  2. Be PASSIONATE to dance! Fuel the stage with your enthusiasm!
  3. Pass the audition held annually in September, showcasing your exceptional dance skills and creativity.
